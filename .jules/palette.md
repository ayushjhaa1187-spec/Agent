## 2024-03-16 - Focus Outlines and Decorative Emojis
**Learning:** Using `currentColor` for `:focus-visible` outlines ensures dynamic contrast across varying backgrounds, and `aria-hidden="true"` prevents redundant screen reader announcements for decorative emojis.
**Action:** Apply `currentColor` for focus states and `aria-hidden="true"` to decorative icons/emojis across all future UX enhancements.
